Exiting Youth Leaders/Office

With a mix of deep gratitude and bittersweet emotion, we announce that our previous Youth Office will be concluding their ministry soon. For the past 1 years, They have poured their hearts and souls into our members. They have been more than just leaders; they have been mentors, friends, and passionate guides who have pointed our youth toward Jesus through every kesha night, youth rallies, and small group discussion. Their impact is etched into the lives of countless young people. We will always remember and be thankful for:

-Their unwavering commitment to our own projects

.

-The 2025 Youth Week project Implementation

While we will miss them tremendously, we send them off with our deepest blessings, immense thanks, and full confidence that God has incredible plans for them ahead.

Celebrating a Season of Youth Ministry Impact

As we welcome a new team to lead our youth, we are compelled to pause and celebrate the mighty work God did through our previous youth leaders. Their faithful service from September 2024 to October 2025 has left a lasting mark on our church and the kingdom of God. We believe in giving thanks where it is due.

A Legacy That Continues

The foundation they built is strong. The relationships they forged are lasting. And the members they discipled are now stronger in their faith. For all of this, we say Thank You to the former Leadership and Praise God for His unwavering faithfulness. The best way we can honor this legacy is to continue supporting our youth ministry with the same passion and commitment. Let's step into this new season with expectation, building on the strong foundation that has been laid.
